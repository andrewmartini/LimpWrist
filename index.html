Limpwrist

Limp Wrist mapping project

Photo by Cristian Lois, 2010.

I am attempting to create a map of all Limp Wrist concerts since our first show in 1999. This is a map created in Cartodb, synching up with a Google table/database. This list is not *yet* exhaustive, but contains over 110 shows. Some of the locations are only approximate (i.e. city, no venue name). As I go, I'm adding some links to the show flyers, and videos hosted on youtube. Eventually, the goal is to either have a photo of the venue or a still from the concert in the infowindow header.

For music check the Limp Wrist bandcamp site.
