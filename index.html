Limpwrist

Limp Wrist mapping project

Photo by Cristian Lois, 2010.

I am attempting to create a list of all Limp Wrist concerts since our first show in 1999. Below is the result of geocoded address, created in Cartodb, synching up with my Google sheet/database. Although not *yet* exhaustive, it contains over 120 shows. Some of the locations are only approximations (i.e. city only, no venue name). Eventually, the goal is to have a photo from the concert in the infowindow (click on point) header, as well as a link to the flyer for the event and online video footage. Wish me luck!
